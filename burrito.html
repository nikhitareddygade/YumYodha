Green Salad

Greek salad, also known as Horiatiki salad, is a refreshing and vibrant dish originating from Greece. It typically features fresh and crisp ingredients such as tomatoes, cucumbers, red onions, bell peppers, Kalamata olives, and feta cheese. The salad is dressed simply with extra virgin olive oil, red wine vinegar or lemon juice, oregano, salt, and pepper, highlighting the flavors of the Mediterranean.

Nutritional Values

Calories: Around 150-200 kcal

Protein: 5-7 grams

Carbohydrates: 10-15 grams

Fat: 10-15 grams

Fiber: 3-4 grams
