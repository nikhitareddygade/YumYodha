Caramel Brownies

Caramel brownies are decadent treats that combine the rich flavors of chocolate and caramel. They typically feature a dense and fudgy chocolate brownie base with a layer of gooey caramel swirled throughout or drizzled on top. These indulgent desserts are loved for their sweet and luxurious taste, making them a favorite for chocolate and caramel enthusiasts alike.

Protein: 2-4 grams

Carbohydrates: 25-35 grams
