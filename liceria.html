Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza is a beloved classic topped with slices of spicy, cured pork or beef sausage, melted mozzarella cheese, and tangy tomato sauce on a crispy or chewy crust. It's a favorite worldwide for its savory and satisfying flavors.
