French Toast

French toast is a classic breakfast dish made from sliced bread soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk, then fried until golden brown. It is typically flavored with cinnamon, vanilla, and sometimes sugar, giving it a sweet and comforting taste. French toast can be served with a variety of toppings such as maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh fruits, or whipped cream, making it a versatile and beloved breakfast option.
