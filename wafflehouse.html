Mini Banana Pancakes

Mini banana pancakes are a delightful twist on traditional pancakes, featuring mashed ripe bananas mixed into the batter for added sweetness and flavor. These small-sized pancakes are typically made with a mixture of flour, milk (or buttermilk), eggs, baking powder, and a touch of sugar. They are cooked on a griddle or skillet until golden brown and fluffy. Mini banana pancakes are often served with maple syrup, honey, fresh berries, or a dollop of yogurt for a delicious and wholesome breakfast or snack.

Nutritional Values

Protein: 5-8 grams

Carbohydrates: 30-40 grams
