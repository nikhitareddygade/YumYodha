Frequently Asked Questions

What is this website about?

Our website helps users discover delicious food by recommending dishes from various restaurants. We also showcase the top trending foods and restaurants to help you stay updated with the latest food trends.

How does the food recommendation system work?

Our bot takes input from users about their food preferences, dietary restrictions, and current cravings. Based on this information, it suggests dishes from local restaurants that match your tastes.

How do I use the recommendation bot?

Simply type in your food preferences or cravings into the chat box on our website. The bot will analyze your input and provide personalized food recommendations from nearby restaurants.

Can I specify dietary restrictions or allergies to the bot?

Yes, you can mention any dietary restrictions, allergies, or preferences when interacting with the bot. It will take these into account to provide safe and suitable recommendations.

What kind of food preferences can I enter?

You can specify any type of cuisine, dish, or specific ingredients you like or dislike. For example, you can ask for recommendations for vegetarian dishes, gluten-free options, spicy foods, or specific cuisines like Italian or Japanese.

How are the top trending foods and restaurants determined?

Our trending list is curated based on user feedback, ratings, and the popularity of dishes and restaurants in our community. We continuously update this list to reflect the latest trends.
