Carbonara Pasta

Carbonara pasta is a classic Italian dish known for its rich and creamy sauce typically made with eggs, cheese (usually Pecorino Romano or Parmesan), pancetta or guanciale (cured pork cheek or bacon), and black pepper. This dish is celebrated for its simplicity and robust flavors, making it a favorite among pasta enthusiasts worldwide.
