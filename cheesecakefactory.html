Cheese Cake

Cheesecake is a decadent dessert known for its creamy and rich texture, typically made with a crust of crushed cookies or graham crackers and a filling composed of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs. Cheesecake is enjoyed worldwide for its indulgent taste and versatility in variations, making it a beloved treat for special occasions and everyday enjoyment alike.
