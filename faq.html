What is sushi?

Sushi is a Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients such as seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.

What is the national dish of Spain?

The national dish of Spain is Paella, a rice dish originally from Valencia. It can include various combinations of vegetables and meats, seafood, and sometimes beans.

What are some popular street foods in Mexico?

Popular street foods in Mexico include:
  • Tacos
  • Elote (grilled corn on the cob)
  • Quesadillas
  • Churros
  • Tamales

What is a traditional British breakfast?

A traditional British breakfast typically includes eggs, bacon, sausages, beans, tomatoes, mushrooms, toast, and sometimes black pudding.

What is kimchi?

Kimchi is a traditional Korean dish made from fermented vegetables, most commonly napa cabbage and Korean radishes, with a variety of seasonings including chili powder, garlic, ginger, and salted seafood.

What are some common ingredients in Indian curry?

Common ingredients in Indian curry include:
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Tomatoes
  • Various spices (e.g., turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili powder)
  • Yogurt or coconut milk (depending on the region)
  • Fresh herbs like cilantro
